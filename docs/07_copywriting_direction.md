# PPM – Pro Performance Maintenance
## Copywriting Direction

---

## 1. Purpose of This Document

This document defines the copywriting direction for the PPM website.

It should be used as the reference for:
- headlines,
- section copy,
- service descriptions,
- CTA labels,
- trust messaging,
- project descriptions,
- testimonial framing,
- contact copy,
- SEO content,
- prompts for AI-generated website copy.

The copy should position PPM as a premium finishing, painting and maintenance partner for Sydney residential, commercial and strata properties.

---

## 2. Core Copywriting Goal

The copy must help visitors quickly understand:

```text
Who PPM is.
What PPM does.
Who PPM works with.
Why PPM is trustworthy.
How to request a quote.
```

The copy should make PPM feel premium, reliable and professional without sounding exaggerated or generic.

---

## 3. Brand Voice

PPM’s voice should be:

- refined,
- professional,
- clear,
- calm,
- confident,
- direct,
- organised,
- practical,
- detail-oriented,
- trustworthy.

The voice should not sound:
- cheap,
- desperate,
- loud,
- gimmicky,
- overly corporate,
- robotic,
- casual handyman-style,
- full of empty marketing buzzwords.

---

## 4. Main Positioning Statement

Preferred positioning:

```text
PPM is a premium painting, finishing and maintenance partner for Sydney residential, commercial and strata properties.
```

Alternative positioning:

```text
Premium painting, plastering, carpentry, strata and remedial maintenance services for Sydney properties.
```

Short version:

```text
Premium finishing and maintenance for Sydney properties.
```

---

## 5. Core Message Pillars

All copy should reinforce these pillars:

### 1. Premium Finish

PPM delivers clean, precise and long-lasting finishes.

### 2. Professional Process

PPM works with preparation, organisation and clear communication.

### 3. Residential and Commercial Trust

PPM is suitable for homeowners, property investors, builders, real estate agencies, property managers, strata managers and commercial clients.

### 4. Practical Trade Expertise

PPM performs real work across painting, plastering, carpentry, strata and remedial repairs.

### 5. Clean and Reliable Delivery

PPM values tidy work, punctuality, care and property respect.

## 7. Words and Phrases to Avoid

Avoid:

```text
cheap
budget
lowest price
handyman
we do everything
no job too small
best in town
amazing deals
unbeatable prices
fastest service guaranteed
construction experts for everything
your one-stop shop
```

Avoid sounding like:
- a generic handyman site,
- a low-cost contractor,
- a SaaS template,
- a corporate brochure,
- a pushy sales page.

---

## 8. Headline Style

Headlines should be:
- short,
- confident,
- premium,
- clear,
- service-focused,
- easy to understand.

Preferred headline structure:
```text
[Premium result] for [audience/property type].
```

Examples:
```text
Premium painting, finishing and maintenance for Sydney properties.
Residential and commercial projects, finished with precision.
A cleaner process. A better finish.
Built on preparation. Finished with precision.
Professional maintenance for homes, strata and commercial properties.
```

Avoid:
```text
We are the best painting company in Sydney!
No job too big or small!
Affordable handyman solutions for everyone!
```

## 23. Microcopy Direction

Use clear microcopy.

Examples:

```text
Service Required
Property Type
Preferred Contact Method
Upload photos of the work area
Tell us what needs attention
We’ll get back to you with the next steps
```

Error states should be polite and clear:

```text
Please enter your phone number.
Please select a service.
Please add a short message about the work required.
```

---

## 24. Final Copywriting Principle

Every line of copy should reinforce:

```text
PPM is professional, organised, premium and trusted to improve, repair and maintain Sydney properties.
```

If a sentence sounds generic, cheap, vague or exaggerated, rewrite it.
